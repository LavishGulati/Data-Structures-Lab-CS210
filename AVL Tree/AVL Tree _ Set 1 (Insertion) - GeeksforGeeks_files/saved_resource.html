          Sanket Mathur Damoy 7 days ago

          The order [10,2,1,25,33] is correct based on your example.
          The tree after every insertion step will be like this:
          1)........10

          2)........10
          ..............\
          ...............25

          3)........10
          .........../....\
          .........2....25

          4)........10
          .........../....\
          ..........2.....25
          ........./
          .......1

          5)........10
          .........../....\
          ..........2.....25
          ........./ ........ \
          .......1........... 33

          Preorder traversal will print the sequence in the order: Root - Left - Right
          It will start from 10, and prints 10. = [10]
          Then it will go to the left of 10 which is on 2. Perform preorder on 2.
          Prints 2. = [10, 2]
          Then go to left of 2 and perform preorder on 1.
          Prints 1. = [10, 2, 1]
          Left of 1 is null. Right part of 1 is also null. Trace back.
          Goes to right of 2., which is null. Trace back
          Goes to 10 again. Right of 10 is 25. Perform preorder on 25.
          Print 25 = [10, 2, 1, 25]
          Go to the left of 25 which is null. Therefore go to right of 25 which is 33. Perform preorder on 33.
          Print 33 = [10, 2, 1, 25, 33]

          Final output: [10, 2, 1, 25, 33]

          Hope that helps.
